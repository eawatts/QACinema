About Us

We are a world leading cinema chain that aim to bring the best visuals and sound to all your favourite movies. Started in 1989 in Manchester and have grown to be all across the UK.

QA Cinemas around the UK offer a range of content, in addition to highly anticipated blockbusters, with event cinema titles including opera, ballet, musicals, national theatre, live streaming of sporting events and concerts through to hosting Gaming championships and dedicating multiple screens to eGaming arenas.
